BUSINESS SET UP

Steps In Business Setup


One to One Consultation with Business Setup Advisor

Schedule a meeting or Zoom call with our advisors to discuss your business activity, shareholders, target customers, office space, and growth opportunities. We’ll help ensure your business plan is detailed, accurate, and ready for implementation.

Selecting The Right Jurisdiction

We can help you choose the best jurisdiction for your business, whether it’s the UAE Mainland for flexibility, a Free Zone for cost-effectiveness, or offshore for global access.

Applying for Trade License

Organize your registration documents, including Application Form, Memorandum of Association, and other essentials. Our team will guide you through the process based on your license type and jurisdiction.

Initiating Visa and Emirates ID Applications

Apply for visas for yourself, your team, and dependents before moving to the UAE. Our team will guide you through the visa application process and provide necessary information.

Opening a Corporate Bank Account

A corporate bank account is essential for doing business in the UAE. We’ll help you select the best bank and prepare the required documentation for a smooth process.




Choose Your Jurisdiction

Create your company in the Mainland and have the flexibility to do business across the UAE.

Select the perfect free zone in the UAE for your business activity from a diverse range of over 50 options.

Do business in the UAE from anywhere, without needing to be physically present. Enjoy a beneficial business environment similar to Mainland and Free Zone, but with a more convenient setup.
